What Happened This Week

3 SENIOR LEADERS KILLED IN 36 HOURS
Larijani (SNSC Secretary, de facto leader) confirmed killed by Iran. Intelligence Minister Khatib killed overnight March 17-18. Basij commander Soleimani + ~300 Basij commanders eliminated. IDF now authorized to kill senior Iranians without prior approval.
SOUTH PARS GAS FIELD STRUCK
first upstream energy target. IAF hit gas treatment plants (Phases 3-5-6) at Asaluyeh, Bushehr. ~1/5 of Iran's gas processing capacity damaged/offline. Iran threatens named Gulf targets in retaliation: Samref Refinery, Jubail Petrochemical, Al Hasan Gas Field.
LEBANON GROUND INVASION
3-5 IDF divisions launched largest ground op since 2006 (March 16). Tyre evacuation ordered. IDF says troops will remain 'for months.' Beirut strikes: 6-20 killed in Bashoura. 922+ killed, 1M+ displaced in Lebanon. 5 NATO allies oppose.
KENT RESIGNS + TRUMP THREATENS NATO
NCTC Director Joe Kent quit (first senior Trump official): 'war due to pressure from Israel and its powerful American lobby.' Trump threatened NATO withdrawal after all allies refused Hormuz coalition. Near-universal rejection: EU, Germany, Japan, Australia declined.
BRENT $106-109, GAS $3.84, DIESEL $5.04
despite 400M bbl strategic release (largest ever). Fed expected to hold rates. Iran offering yuan-denominated Hormuz transit to 8 countries. First non-Iranian tanker (Karachi) transited with AIS March 15. 20,000 seafarers stranded.
MOJTABA 'WOUNDED AND DISFIGURED'
Hegseth assessment. Still no public appearance (20 days). Banking crisis (Melli/Sepah failures) ahead of Nowruz March 20. Internet blackout Day 18+, AS12880 network collapsed. 500 detained as alleged spies. Iran executes Swedish citizen.
ALL ALLIES REFUSE HORMUZ COALITION
EU: 'no appetite, this is not Europe's war.' Germany explicitly declined. Trump 'greatly disappointed.' First non-Iranian tanker (Aframax Karachi) transited Hormuz with AIS on March 15.
