What Happened This Week

LEBANON GROUND INVASION LAUNCHED
Defense Minister Katz confirmed IDF 'targeted ground operation' in southern Lebanon. Displaced Lebanese 'will not return south of Litani.' Hezbollah claimed 31 attacks + 7 ATGM engagements in 24 hours. 850+ dead, 900,000 displaced. Largest Israeli ground operation since 2006.
RUSSIA CONFIRMED SUPPLYING WEAPONS TO IRAN
Zelensky: drones with 'Russian details.' Iran purchased 500 Verbas MANPADS + 2,500 9M336 missiles (Dec 2025). Araghchi confirmed 'close cooperation including military assistance' from Russia + China. Escalation ladder: intel → tactics → weapons.
BOTH SIDES REJECT CEASEFIRE
Araghchi: 'we don't ask for ceasefire... war must end so enemies never repeat.' Trump: 'terms aren't good enough.' All mediation rejected. CNN: 'If Trump already won, why does he need foreign ships?' FCC threatens broadcaster licenses over negative coverage.
MOJTABA 'LOW CONDITION TO FUNCTION'
Israeli intel says IRGC personnel 'managing him,' injured. CBS: US believes IRGC 'calling the shots.' Mass desertions, Basij faking deaths, 500 arrested for sending info to adversaries. 18 arrested as alleged Israeli 'mercenaries.'
FUJAIRAH HIT AGAIN
2nd drone strike in 3 days, oil loading re-suspended. Dubai airport drone fire. Iran systematically targeting UAE's Hormuz bypass. Oil: Brent $104-105, WTI ~$100, gas $3.70/gal (+24%). Record $211M meme-style oil ETF trading.
IRGC SUPPLY CRISIS
some units down to 20 bullets per 2 soldiers (Iran International). Artesh-IRGC rift deepening: IRGC refused to transport wounded regular army soldiers to hospitals. Basij dispersing from checkpoints to highway underpasses after internal security strikes. 20 arrested for 'cooperating with Israel.'
SCHOOL STRIKE: CONFIRMED US TOMAHAWK
Pentagon preliminary investigation found outdated DIA intelligence (school was IRGC base ~15 years ago). Formal 15-6 investigation ordered. 170-180 killed. March 18 deadline in 3 days. Trump still blaming Iran.
