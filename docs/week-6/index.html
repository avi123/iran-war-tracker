What Happened This Week

FIRST US AIRCRAFT LOSSES
F-15E Strike Eagle shot down over southwestern Iran (April 3), first US fixed-wing loss to enemy fire since 2003. Both crew rescued by April 5 in joint military/CIA operation from Iranian mountains. A-10 also downed near Strait same day. Rescue cost 2 C-130s + 2 MH-6Ms destroyed to prevent capture. Iran offered $60K bounty for crew.
Persisted across 2 update cycles
HOUTHIS ENTERED THE WAR (March 28)
BM launched at Beersheba (intercepted). 5+ attacks on Israel through April 2. Claiming 'joint coordination' with Iran, Hezbollah, Iraqi PMF. Threatening Bab el-Mandeb naval blockade. Axis of resistance now fully activated across 4 fronts simultaneously.
Persisted across 2 update cycles
DIPLOMACY AT DEAD END
5-day pause expired, energy strike pause extended to April 6. US 15-point plan rejected as 'maximalist.' Iran's 5 conditions rejected. 48-hour ceasefire rejected. Pakistan mediation failed. Mediators told WSJ: 'dead end.' China-Pakistan ceasefire proposal emerged (new track). Trump: 'deal possible by Monday' while also 'no deal needed.'
Persisted across 2 update cycles
CASUALTY SURGE
Iran: 2,076+ killed (Health Ministry) to 3,519 (Al Monitor), 244+ children, 26,500+ wounded. US: 13 KIA, 365 WIA. Israel: 19 civilians + 10 IDF in Lebanon dead, 6,833 hospital evacuations. Lebanon: 1,001+ killed, 1M+ displaced (20% of population). 195 cities across 27 provinces struck. 6 political prisoners executed.
Persisted across 2 update cycles
TWO-WEEK CEASEFIRE AGREED
Pakistan-brokered deal announced ~90 minutes before Trump's 8PM deadline April 7. Iran's SNSC accepted. China provided 'last-minute nudge.' US/Israel suspend all strikes on Iran for 14 days. Iran agrees to 'complete, immediate, safe opening' of Hormuz. Iran + Oman collect transit tolls. Islamabad talks begin Friday April 10 (Witkoff, Kushner, Vance). Iraq's Islamic Resistance declared parallel halt.
PASSOVER ATTACK CONFIRMED + OIL CRASHED
Iran fired largest missile salvo in weeks during Seder night: ~20+ BMs including cluster munitions. Coordinated with Hezbollah (100+ rockets) + Houthis (drones at Eilat). 4 killed Haifa, 11-year-old critically hurt. Post-ceasefire: oil crashed ~16% (WTI to $94.55, Brent to $94.13) — still 30% above pre-war. Markets surged (S&P +2.7%, Nikkei +5.4%).
CASUALTIES UPDATED
Iran: 3,597 killed per HRANA (most methodical: 1,665 civilians, 1,221 military, 711 unclassified). Lebanon: 1,497-1,530 killed, 1.2M displaced (20% of population). Israel: 23 civilians + 12 soldiers killed, 7,183 civ + 411 mil wounded. US: 15 KIA, 538 WIA. Gulf: 28 dead. Post-ceasefire Gulf attacks April 8: UAE, Kuwait (28 drones intercepted), Bahrain, Saudi pipeline all hit.
