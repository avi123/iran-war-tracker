What Happened This Week

TRUMP 48-HOUR HORMUZ ULTIMATUM
demands Iran 'FULLY OPEN the Strait within 48 HOURS' or US will 'obliterate' power plants 'STARTING WITH THE BIGGEST ONE FIRST.' Iran counter-threatened 'irreversible' destruction of regional energy, IT, and desalination infrastructure. IRGC: complete Hormuz closure if energy sites hit. Same weekend Trump said US 'considering winding down.'
DIMONA/ARAD INTERCEPT FAILURE
Iranian Khorramshahr-4 missiles struck Arad and Dimona (March 21-22), first targeting of Israel's nuclear zone. ~180 wounded, 0 killed. Israeli air defenses failed — IAF called failure to intercept two separate missiles 'coincidental.' Iran framed as retaliation for Natanz strike. IAEA: no damage to Dimona reactor.
NUCLEAR TIT-FOR-TAT
Natanz struck 2nd time with bunker-busters (March 21). IAEA confirmed damage to entrance buildings, no radiation leak. Iran retaliated by targeting Dimona. IAEA chief Grossi: war 'can't entirely eliminate' Iran's nuclear program. New Isfahan underground facility remains uninspected. Nuclear facility targeting now mutual.
IRGC JUNTA CONSOLIDATING
Mojtaba still absent (14+ days). Government confirmed injured in airstrikes. IRGC troika (Vahidi/Ghalibaf/Jafari) filling vacuum. Israeli officials: IRGC 'mainly filled the power vacuum.' Missile production reduced from ~100 BMs/month to zero. 3 protesters publicly executed in Qom — first from January detainees. Iran FM: 'no reason to negotiate now.' Saudi expelled Iranian diplomats.
DIEGO GARCIA IRBM
Iran launched 2 intermediate-range ballistic missiles at US-UK base ~4,000km away (March 21). One failed mid-flight, one intercepted. Neither hit. Confirmed by UK MoD and IDF; CENTCOM has not officially commented. Iran denied responsibility. IDF Chief Zamir: 'Berlin, Paris, Rome all within direct threat range.'
CENTCOM: 8,000+ TARGETS, 130+ VESSELS
ADM Cooper Day 22 update: 'largest elimination of a navy since WWII.' 8,000+ combat flights. A-10s and Apaches deployed (= Iranian AD threat functionally neutralized). IDF Chief Zamir: campaign 'approximately halfway through' — implies ~6-week total, through Passover. 200+ targets struck over weekend in simultaneous waves on Iran + Lebanon.
