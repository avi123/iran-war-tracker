Assessment

The attrition picture has shifted dramatically since Day 24. Iran retains ~50% of missile launchers and ~1,000 missiles (CNN/Haaretz) — far more than 'near-exhaustion' previously assessed. Missile volume is down 90%+ but Iran has shifted to quality over quantity: fewer launches, better targeting of US enabler infrastructure (AWACS, radar, SATCOM, tankers). Israel believes Iran 'can keep firing indefinitely.' Meanwhile, Israel's Arrow stocks are ~80% depleted (RUSI estimate: ~122 of ~150 expended). Haifa BM intercept failed April 5. Replacement: 2-3 years even with accelerated production. The F-15E + A-10 losses (April 3) demonstrate Iran retains lethal air defense capability despite 80%+ AD destruction. The attrition equation has become more dangerous: Iran is adapting faster than it's depleting.
