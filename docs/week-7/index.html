What Happened This Week

ISLAMABAD TALKS UNDERWAY
First direct US-Iran talks since 1979. VP Vance + Kushner leading US delegation; Ghalibaf (71-person team) + Araghchi for Iran. Pakistan PM Sharif mediating. Immediate impasse: Ghalibaf set preconditions (Lebanon ceasefire + frozen asset release) before substantive talks. Trump: 'they have no cards,' 'no nuclear weapon is 99% of the deal.' Iran claims US agreed to release frozen assets — unconfirmed by US side. FDD: US 'completely discarded' Iran's 10-point proposal. Arms Control Association: US negotiators 'ill-prepared for serious nuclear talks.'
LEBANON DEATH TOLL ~1,950
'Operation Eternal Darkness' April 8: 50 jets, 160 bombs, 100 targets in 10 minutes, 357 killed (1,223 injured). Civilian/militant ratio disputed — IDF claimed 180 Hezbollah among dead, observers note strikes hit commercial zones during rush hour without warning. IDF killed 8 Lebanese security personnel April 10. IDF taking Bint Jbail (98th Division). Israel excluded France from Lebanon talks. Israel Hayom: 'Lebanon will determine the fate of the campaign.' Ghalibaf's Lebanon ceasefire precondition is the primary diplomatic friction point.
ENRICHMENT DEADLOCK TOTAL
Iran's nuclear chief Eslami publicly rejected ALL enrichment restrictions: 'demands to restrict Iran's enrichment programme are merely wishes that will be buried.' Trump: 'no nuclear weapon is 99% of the deal.' US proposal: hand over HEU + dismantle enrichment. Iran position: enrichment is sovereign right, non-negotiable. Positions have zero overlap. Fordow undamaged. 440 kg HEU unverifiable. IAEA: zero access since Day 1.
HORMUZ MINE CRISIS
NYT/Haaretz: Iran cannot find or remove its own mines in the Strait. Only 4 tanker transits/day vs 135 normal. 400+ tankers anchored outside Gulf. Iran's parliament legislating toll system in rials, banning 'hostile' shipping. EU warned of airport jet fuel crunch within 3 weeks. IRGC using ceasefire to tighten Hormuz grip. Even good-faith reopening faces physical obstacles — mines are uncontrolled.
CENTCOM: 'GENERATIONAL MILITARY DEFEAT'
Admiral Cooper: 13,000+ strikes, 50,000+ US troops, '40-year military build-up crushed in under 40 days.' 70%+ BM launchers disabled. BUT: China preparing MANPADs (shoulder-fired anti-air missiles) shipment to Iran routed through third countries (CNN exclusive). Kuwait intercepted 7 hostile drones in 24 hours during ceasefire — proxy forces still active. Haaretz: 'Trump wants out and Netanyahu is extremely disappointed.'
REGIME FRACTURE NOW PHYSICAL
Basij members stoned FM Araghchi outside Foreign Ministry, protesting his Islamabad participation. Hardliner rally against Ghalibaf: 'Curse you if you trample on the blood of the Leader.' Pezeshkian: 'Iran will never abandon Lebanon.' OpIndia: IRGC described as 'de facto ruler' — Mojtaba absent 35+ days. Internet blackout 41+ days (960+ hours). Iranians celebrated ceasefire in streets. 857 schools, 32 universities, 338 hospitals damaged/destroyed (NPR). US CPI surged to 3.3% — war-driven inflation.
